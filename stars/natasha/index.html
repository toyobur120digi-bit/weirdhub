Scarlett Johansson

About Scarlett

Scarlett Ingrid Johansson is an American actress known for her versatility and powerful performances. Born on November 22, 1984, she has become one of Hollywood's highest-grossing actresses and a global icon.

She gained worldwide fame for portraying Black Widow in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. With a career spanning over two decades, Scarlett has received multiple award nominations including Academy Awards and Golden Globes.
